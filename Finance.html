Financial Overview

Understanding Your Financial Health

The Financial Overview section shows your clinic's key financial metrics at a glance:

  • Total Revenue: All income from dental services
  • Outstanding Balance: Payments still due from patients
  • Net Income: Revenue minus expenses
  • Active Patients: Current patient count
üìà Interactive Chart: The chart below shows monthly trends. Try changing the time period using the dropdown!
Each card shows percentage changes to help you track performance trends.

Financial Reports

Access Detailed Financial Reports

Click any of these report cards to view detailed financial information:

  • Income Report: Complete financial summary with revenue breakdown
  • Installment Plan Report: Track patient payment plans and schedules
  • Per Dentist Report: Revenue analysis by dentist
  • Patients with Balance: Outstanding payments and collections
üíé Hover Effect: Try hovering over the report cards to see the interactive animations!

Step 3 of 8

Adding Payments

Recording Patient Payments

Use the "Add Payment" button to record new patient payments:

  1. Select the patient from the dropdown
  2. Choose payment type (Full, Installment, Down Payment)
  3. Enter the amount paid
  4. Select payment method (Cash, GCash, Bank Transfer)
  5. Add optional remarks for tracking
‚ö° Real-time Updates: All payments are automatically tracked and appear in the relevant reports immediately.
The system will automatically update all financial metrics and charts in real-time.

Custom Report Generator

Creating Custom Reports

The Custom Report Generator allows you to create tailored reports:

  • Date Range: Select time period (Today, Week, Month, Quarter, Year)
  • Report Type: Filter by Income, Installments, or Balance
  • Dentist: Filter reports by specific dentist (optional)
  • Generate Report: Click to create your custom report
üîç Try It Now: Select "This Week" and "Income Only" then click "Generate Report" to see it in action!
This is useful for specific analysis or when preparing financial statements for specific periods.
Step 5 of 8

Generated Reports

Understanding Generated Reports

When you generate a custom report, it appears in this section with:

  • Report Summary: Key statistics and totals at the top
  • Detailed Table: All filtered transactions with hover effects
  • Filter Information: Shows what filters were applied
  • Export Options: You can print or save the report
üìä Data Insights: Hover over table rows to highlight them. Click column headers to sort data.
Use these reports for monthly analysis, tracking specific dentist performance, monitoring payments, and preparing tax documents.
