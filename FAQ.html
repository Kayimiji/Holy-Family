Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Answers to common questions about appointments, services, payments and clinic policies. If you don't find what you're looking for, contact us and we'll help.

What is SMART DENTISTRY?

SMART DENTISTRY is a web-based system designed to manage patient dental and oral health records, appointments, and clinic data in dental clinics located in Población, Santa Maria, Bulacan.

What problem does SMART DENTISTRY aim to solve?

The system addresses issues caused by manual record-keeping, such as slow data retrieval, misplaced records, inaccurate patient information, scheduling conflicts, and weak data security.

Who are the intended users of the system?

The system is intended for dentists, dental assistants, clinic staff, and patients who interact with dental records and appointment services.

What types of records can be managed using SMART DENTISTRY?

The system manages patient personal information, dental and medical history, treatment records, appointment schedules, and clinic reports in a standardized digital format.

How does the system ensure data security and privacy?

SMART DENTISTRY applies user authentication, encrypted storage, controlled access, and consent-based data sharing, in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173).

What are the limitations of SMART DENTISTRY?

The system is limited to small to medium dental clinics in Población, Santa Maria, Bulacan and does not include advanced features such as online payments, teledentistry, or inventory management.
